Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that in run by an all-volunteer board of director. Our mission is to bring national and international renowned, thought-provoking speaker who inform, educate, and entertain our andience! As one or our members told us:

“Each year I give a ticket package to each of our family members. I think of it as the gift of knowlegde...and that is priceless”

Speaker of the Month

Fossil Thread in the Web of Life

February

Scott Sampson

What's 75 million years old and brand spanking new? A teenage Utahcecratops! Come to the Saroyan, armed with your best dinosaur roar, when Scott Sampson, Research Curator at the Utah Museum of Natural History, steps to the podium. Sampson's research has focused on the ecology and evolution of late Cretaceous dinosaurs and he has conducted fieldwork in a number of countries in Africa.
